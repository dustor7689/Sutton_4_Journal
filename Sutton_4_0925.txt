Definitions:

Paid Licencse- having to pay to have the right to use a software.
Shareware- Software that has been legally shared.
Trailware- A temporary right to use a software  
Ad-ware- The downloading or pop-ups of ads
Crippleware- Software given that has reduced functionality with the thought of attracting money for the full product.
Freemium- Free base product with addons that cost money.
Freeware (vs Free-OpenSource)- Free-OpenSource is free and editable while freeware is just free software.
Richard Stallman- He is a free software deeloper.
Lawrence Lessig- Politician and lawyer supported free software.
Eben Moglen- Professor of law and supporter of free software.
